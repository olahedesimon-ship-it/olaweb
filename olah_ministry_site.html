About Me

A Life Poured Out for God's Kingdom

I am Pastor Olah Ede Simon — a Pastor, Teacher, and Mentor with a divine mandate to build believers into the express image of Christ. Through my ministry platform, Lovefold Pastors Care, I am dedicated to restoring ministers and helping them rediscover the joy of their calling.

My passion for sound doctrine and practical discipleship is evident in all my writings. As a Christian Content Editor and Ministry Trainer, I help the Church communicate the Gospel clearly and powerfully — both in print and in the digital space.

My calling is simple: to raise men and women who are the express image of God in their generation, and to equip pastors to serve with renewed passion and lasting Kingdom impact.
